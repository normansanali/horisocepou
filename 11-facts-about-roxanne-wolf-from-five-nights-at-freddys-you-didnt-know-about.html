11 Facts About Roxanne Wolf From 'Five Nights At Freddy's' You Didn't Know About

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  860 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Roxanne Wolf is a character from Five Nights at Freddy’s: Security Breach and the Ruin DLC, where she starts as a secondary antagonist but her role shifts and she becomes a minor supporting deuteragonist. Additionally, she makes frequent appearances in Tales from the Pizza Plex.

The much-anticipated Five Nights At Freddy’s has been taken to a whole new level with the addition of Security Breach. This latest installment brings twice the thrills and chills with its time shift and the introduction of new characters, such as Roxanne Wolf.

Facts About Roxanne Wolf

Roxanne Wolf was one of the four robotic characters representing Freddy Fazbear’s Mega Pizzaplex. She acted as a mascot for Roxy’s Raceway.

1. Her wardrobe has a 1980s vibe

Roxanne’s clothing has an 80s vibe, just like the other members of Glamrocks. She is wearing:

  • A red crop top, pants, and shoulder pads decorated with black star-like designs.
  • Arms and legs colored in a deep shade of purple, adorned with a black tiger pattern.
  • Both arms are adorned with black studded wristbands.
  • A collar and belt with studs.
  • A hoop earring in one ear and a studded one on the other.

2. Roxanne Wolf’s natural look has an impressive array of colors.

The animatronic generally has a grey wolf-like shape, with yellow eyes. Her hair is silver with green streaks, while her tail is grey with a silver end. Additionally, her inner ears, muzzle, fingers, and front torso all have a silvery color. In addition to these hues, other pigments include:

  • Green hand claws
  • Purple feet claws
  • Purple eyelids and lipstick
  • Black mascara
  • Black stripped cheeks

3. Roxanne was hit by a mini-kart

She sustained a significant injury when she was struck by a mini-kart while participating in the Roxy Raceway, causing her a great deal of harm. After the incident, Gregory takes her eyes, and her face is mangled, with the centermost part having a large jagged hole. Her jaw became limp.

Furthermore, large sections of her external shell are missing, leaving her endoskeleton exposed. Her mane is knotted and her body is smudged with oil and grime. Her ashen tail has been almost completely severed, showing her interior endoskeleton and wiring.

4. The events of the Security Breach wrecked her.

The mini-kart has done serious harm to Roxy and the Security Breach has exacerbated the situation. As a result, her hull has been very badly damaged, leaving her endoskeleton exposed. Additionally, she has suffered other issues such as:

  • She has no longer got her ears.
  • Her face is replaced by a strange fish-like object
  • Jaws are still loose

5. She has missing fingers in her left hand

When Cassie puts on the Security Mask, amid total destruction, Roxanne’s missing body parts holographically manifest with a brilliant emerald light.

6. Roxanne Wolf is deactivated by Cassie

Cassie is compelled to shut down Roxanne after Gregory reveals to her that she is the final security node needed to rescue him. At this juncture, players have no other choice but to deactivate Roxanne as she is the final Security Node keeping Gregory confined.

After Cassie demolished her beloved animatronic, she was observed expressing sorrow for its destruction and even apologizing to the deactivated Roxanne. As she walked into the final stage, she was seen to be sobbing over the incident.

7. Roxanne has a superiority complex

Roxy begins the game with a great deal of self-confidence. She is often found praising herself when looking in the mirror or other situations.

Due to Glamrock Roxy’s advanced artificial intelligence that is equivalent to humans, she has developed self-esteem issues. This is likely the reason she mocks Gregory; she is displaying her uncertainties.

8. She has a twin sister

In the short movie Roxanne’s Twin Sister, Gregory, and Freddy meet two Roxannes and strive to determine which one is the genuine one. They eventually manage to differentiate the two, with the phony one smashing into a wall and breaking like glass. The real Roxanne is her blue-colored sister.

9. Roxanne Wolf is smitten with Glamrock Foxy

Roxanne Wolf is very fond of Glamrock Foxy, who was introduced in the Security Breach: Ruin DLC during Roxy’s Raceway. Despite being a robotic character, Glamrock Foxy was not part of the main stage cast, instead, he acted as an aide in the daycare.

Foxy finds a place he can call home when he visits the Mega Pizza plex in his Glamrock Animatronic form. He quickly develops strong feelings for Roxy, while she too begins to grow fond of him. Along with them, four other friends reside in the same place.

10. Roxanne was rebuilt using Foxy’s parts

Roxy Wolf was constructed with components taken from Glamrock Foxy, who had already been decommissioned when all the Pizza Plex animatronics were manufactured.

11. Roxanne Wolf worked as a blind animatronic

Gregory tricked Cassie into shutting down Roxy, leaving her in an unconscious state. He then extracted her eyes and gave them to Freddy.

Although Roxy is visually impaired, she keeps going through the game as a blind robot. Nevertheless, her situation leads to her being emotionally fragile, which is seen through her intense warnings and regular meltdowns.
