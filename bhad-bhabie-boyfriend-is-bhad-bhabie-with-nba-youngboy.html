The ink has since been covered up but it looks as though her admiration for the street rapper from Baton Rouge remains.

Once, she was even pictured in bed with NBA YoungBoy, starting off a series of rumors and leading them to potentially tone down their shenanigans.

Kentrell DeSean Gaulden known professionally as YoungBoy Never Broke Again (also known as NBA YoungBoy or simply YoungBoy), is an American rapper.

Between 2015 and 2017, he released six independent mixtapes and steadily garnered a cult following through his work.

In late 2017, Gaulden was signed to Atlantic Records. In January 2018, he released the single “Outside Today”, which peaked at number 31 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

The song became the lead single for his debut studio album Until Death Call My Name (2018) which peaked at number 7 on the US Billboard 200.
