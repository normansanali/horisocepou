Brian Ngo Family

Brian Ngo is a son of a Vietnam war refugee who settled in a small rural community on Missouri’s vast plains. Early on, he learned about living between two cultures and the importance of respecting both. He has featured his dad on his Instagram when they went on vacation in Vietnam in 2017.

In August 2019, Brian announced on his Instagram that his grandfather turned 96 years old. He also posted pictures of his parents that featured his older sister and his mother.

Brian Ngo Job

Brian Ngo started his career as Sergeant of Marines in United States Marine Corps in August 2008. He served as a Sergeant of Marines and a Utilities Chief on two overseas deployments for 3/1 & 3/6 infantry battalions for four years till September 2012. After living his military career, he started working for Edelman as a strategy intern.

In January 2015, Brian joined The Marcus Graham Project as an account planner. His job there lasted till September 2015. A month later, he joined Epsilon as a strategist from November 2015 to February 2016. It was followed by his job at Cavalry as an associate strategic planner for six months.

Also, in July 2016, Brian joined Sanders/Wingo as a strategic planner and worked in the company for nine months. He helped develop impactful advertising campaigns & execution that were rooted in behavioral psychology.

From March 2017 to April 2018, Brian worked for Grey Group as a Senior Strategist. Then, he worked from April 2018 to March 2019 as Senior Strategic Planner for Rauxa. In April 2019 Brian joined DDB as a senior strategist. He helped to develop the U.S. Army’s brand and recruiting initiatives.

Furthermore, starting from September 2021, Brian has been serving as Adjunct Professor for Columbia College Chicago and Wunderman Thompson as Associate Strategy Director. His website states that his career has focused on connecting general and multicultural audiences to notable brands like Verizon, P&G, Nestle, U.S. Army, and Samsung.

Moreover, Glassdoor revealed Associate Strategy Director makes nearly $132,836/year as salary.

About his academic qualifications, Brian holds BA in Columbia College Chicago in Public Relations/Image Management. He earned MS in Industrial and Organizational Psychology from Southern New Hampshire University. Furthermore, he secured his Doctorate in Psychology from Touro University Worldwide in Human and Organizational Psychology.
