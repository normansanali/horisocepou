Machine Gun Kelly Recalls Double Date Gone Wrong with Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  348 words  |  Martina Birk

Machine Gun Kelly isn't shy about his bromance with Pete Davidson—or about his thoughts on the Saturday Night Live comedian's current relationship and social media drama.

During a talk on The Howard Stern Show, the singer admitted he was "totally" there for Davidson when Kanye West began making controversial comments about him on social media. "I got thrown into that too, oddly enough. At the end of the day, man, we're young men trying to find our place in the world and figure it out, and it doesn't really help that you have a million voices ripping you apart," MGK said.

He added that he and Davidson "have so much love" to give and they "love everybody," and if that means they "need to be the vessels for someone's anger, then so be it. That must be why we're here."

The singer, who released his album Mainstream Sellout just last week, also opened up on the podcast about a double date gone wrong that he and fiancée Megan Fox had with Davidson and girlfriend Kim Kardashian.

"We rented out a theater," he recalled, "and I was vouching for this movie so hard, I was like, 'It's gonna be the best movie you've ever seen.'" But he said that 10 minutes into the film, they all realized it was a mistake. "Do you ever just have that moment where you're like, 'We shouldn't be here'?" MGK said.

Despite the bad date, MGK said he's "so happy" for Davidson and Kardashian, and revealed the comedian will definitely be by his side when he says "I do" to Fox. "I think Pete's absolutely gonna be standing there with me," MGK said. "We should just mic him for commentary anyway."
