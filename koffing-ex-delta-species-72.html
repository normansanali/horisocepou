Koffing (Japanese: ドガース Dogars) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the EX Delta Species expansion.

Trivia

This card is extremely similar to the Koffing released in the Expedition Base Set. The only differences are Confusion Gas's lack of having to flip a coin for the effect to work, and Ram costing twice as much, with a doubled damage.
