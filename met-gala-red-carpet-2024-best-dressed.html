Met Gala 2024: All the best dressed stars on this years red carpet

 Posted on July 6, 2024  |  9 minutes  |  1722 words  |  Martina Birk

    The Met Gala 2024 is officially underway and some of our favourite famed faces are taking to the Metropolitan Museum of Art steps donning a series of stellar ensembles that fit within this year's dress code and overarching Sleeping Beauties: Reawakening Fashion theme.

    Every year the Met Gala is coined the fashion world's most anticipated event of the year, attracting the likes of Zendaya, Rihanna, Emily Blunt and Rita Ora, just to name a few.

    This year the famous event will attract the eyes of millions from across the globe, all watching closely to see what their favourite stars and designers have been working on over the past few months. 

    Stay tuned as we bring you all the glitz and glam, straight from the Met Gala 2024 red carpet...

    Zendaya

    For her second look of the night, Zendaya transformed into a literal bouquet of flowers. Letting her bold headpiece shine she wore an all-black renaissance-inspired ensemble from Givenchy's SS96 Couture collection.

    Adwoa Aboah

    Making a statement in a H&M red frill crop and low-rise bubble skirt, model and soon-to-be new mother Adwoa Aboah made a serious style statement.


    Kylie Jenner

    Donning a Oscar De La Renta fitted champagne-toned gown with accenting pointed bust detailing, Kylie Jenner no doubt just inspired a wave of brides-to-be. 

    Sydney Sweeney

    'It' girl Sydney Sweeney has traded in her buttercup-blonde bob for a jet-black option to match her leather glove and baby blue princess gown look, accessorised with De Beers jewels.

    Kaia Gerber

    Kaia Gerber made a subtle statement in a custom Prada white strapless gown covered in iridescent disc sequins. 

    Sabrina Carpenter

    Sabrina Carpenter is working late because she's at the Met Gala in the most glamourous Oscar de la Renta black figure-hugging gown with a voluminous baby blue and white hem. 

    Naomi Campbell

    Supermodel royalty Naomi Campbell kept things simple yet elegant in a custom blue-toned glitter fringed dress from Burberry.

    Elizabeth Debicki

    Elizabeth Debicki stunned in a decadent Dior mustard-hued strapless gown and matching floral headpiece.

    Simone Ashley

    Simone Ashley traded in hercorset for a sleek Prabal Gurung navy blue sequin gown with cut-out panel detailing.

    Dua Lipa

    Leaning right into her goth-girl era, Dua Lipa donned a low-rise lace skirt, and white corset with a layer of black lace babydoll top overtop, a pair of sheer black polka dot gloves and an opulent feather coat all designed by Marc Jacobs.

    Lana Del Rey

    An extremely ethereal Lana Del Rey brought a single red rose to accessorise her Alexander McQueen tree root gown.

    Kim Kardashian

    Kim somehow made a grey cardigan look chic, layering it over her custom Maison Margiela Couture metallic silver leaf dress. 

    Kendall Jenner

    Kenny J just made an entrance in an archival 1999 Givenchy black beaded two-piece with scalloped midriff detailing and eccentric fringed shoulder accents.

    Sarah Paulson

    Sarah Paulson took coquette-core to all new heights in her bow-adorned baby pink and black gown from Prada.

    Amanda Seyfried

    Amanda Seyfried decided on a striking custom Prada metallic silver look, complete with a chrome hairstyle and floral tiara.

    Phoebe Dynevor

    Perfecting the 'underwear as outerwear' aesthetic star Phoebe Dynevor made a statement in a sheer blush pink look with floral accents from Victoria Beckham.

    Serena Williams

    Serena Williams made a statement in a glistening gold one-shoulder Balenciaga gown, sheer black tights and long gloves combo.

    Elle Fanning

    Elle Fanning looked like a real-life Disney princess in her glass-slipper-inspired sheer off-the-shoulder Balmain dress, complete with transparent resin birds on her shoulders which look like they helped her get dressed.

    Ariana Grande

    Ari looked like an ethereal dream in her custom Loewe peplum corset and sheer organza skirt combo.

    Demi Moore

    Demi Moore leaned right into this year's theme donning a large floral printed gown with accentuated hip and shoulder detailing from Harris Reed and Cartier High Jewellery.

    Rita Ora

    Wearing a multicoloured beaded Marni top/neckpiece hybrid overtop of a sheer catsuit the British-born songstress made sure to turn heads.

    Jessica Biel

    Jessica Biel opted for a Tamara Ralph Couture plunging neckline gown in a vibrant pink shade for the 2024 Met Gala. Her showstopping ensemble was complete with dainty flower petal accents.

    Greta Lee

    For this evening's event, Greta Lee called upon Loewe to create an artistic gown made from white lace and fitted with sculptural boning detail and De Beers jewellery.

    Emily Ratajkowski

    NYC street-style maven Emily Ratajkowski just proved that the sheer dress trend isn't going anywhere just yet. Stepping out in an Atelier Versace FW01 Haute Couture open-back gown made completely from intricate beads.

    Gigi Hadid

    Our style muse and all-around 'it' girl Gigi Hadid has stepped onto the red carpet in a voluminous Thom Browne off-the-shoulder white gown with bold black piping trim perfectly paired with decorative yellow roses and green stem and leaf designs. 

    Taylor Russell

    Taylor Russell just arrived in a custom-made Loewe wood-looking corset and draped white skirt combo. Her lacquered wooden bust featured two leaf patterns in a darker hue.

    Sarah Jessica Parker

    SJP leaned into her Carrie Bradshaw alter ego in a stunning Richard Quinn nude-toned mini dress fixed with striking lace and diamante detailing. She completed her killer look with a pair of sheer boots, also embellished to perfection with diamantes.

    Josh O'Connor

    Josh O'Connor was clearly influenced by his very stylish co-star Zendaya for this evening affair. The star donned a full Loewe ensemble complete with floral kicks that would be perfect for summer gardening.

    Tyla

    Tyla opted for a fitted look made from actual beach sand. The Balmain ensemble was custom-made to fit the singer's stunning physique.

    Ambika Mod

    star Ambika Mod kept things elegant for her first-ever Met Gala appearance in a strapless Loewe gown printed with a black dress motif on the front.

    Sabrina Harrison

    Sabrina Harrison decided to drip in a glistening gold CHRISHABANA strapless gown with a clock face bustier statement. Completing the look were dozens of watch faces cascading down the ensemble and a striking headpiece.

    Mindy Kaling

    Mindy Kaling has left us breathless in the most elegant, striking and interesting Gaurav Gupta ensemble which has been tailored to fit the actress like a glove.

    Madelyn Cline

    Madelyn Cline boasted big bridal vibes in a Tommy Hilfiger tailored collared blouse which descended into a voluminous satin skirt complete with applique dandelion accents. 

    Greta Gerwig

    Barbie director Greta Gerwig, responsible for the pink trend resurgence we all know and love, made a statement in a black trousers and cape ensemble fixed with black beads from Chloe.

    Lea Michele

    A pregnant Lea Michele arrived in a striking Rodarte blue option with floral detailing on one shoulder. She paired her bump and blue-hued gown with a tulle cape in the same tone.

    Zendaya

    After months of anticipation, Queen Z has arrived on the steps of The Met in a custom Maison Margiela Couture look styled by her BFF and fashion royalty, Law Roach. Keeping on theme for this year's dress code her blue and green look was accessorised with fauna decorations and a velvet green, feathered headpiece. 

    Rebecca Ferguson

    Rebecca Ferguson joined forces with designer Thom Browne to create a sequin black and white longsleeve gown which she wore under a voluminous black cape, lined in satin baby blue and fixed with black bird motifs.

    Jennifer Lopez

    Right on cue, Jennifer Lopez has turned heads in a glistening, crystal-encrusted sheer web-like gown created by none other than custom Schiaparelli's Haute Couture division. 

    Nell Diamond

    Former Fashion Insider Nell Diamond has entered the chat donning a custom-made Hill House Home striking orange sheer gown and multicoloured necklace.

    Lily James

    Lily James has kept things elegant in a blush-pink plunging neckline gown with black floral accents and fixed with a lengthy train from Erdem.

    Bad Bunny

    Co-chair of the evening Bad Bunny decided to dress to impress in a custom Maison Margiela suit with a matching hat and leather gloves.

    Bee Carrozzini

    Anna Wintour's daughter Bee Carrozzini decided on a decadent white and black lace Alexander McQueen look from the famed brand's Summer/Spring 2016 RTW collection. 

    Gwendoline Christie

    Gwendoline Christie of course chose to wear a custom Maison Margiela ensemble. Pairing a striking red velvet gown with an organza black cape and a voluminous hairstyle, Gwendoline just proved exactly why we love this event so much.

    Emma Chamberlain

    Queen of the social media sphere Emma Chamberlain has arrived in a Jean Paul Gaultier sheer-lace strapless gown, complete with a floating cape and gloves.

    Anna Wintour

    Co-chair of this year's Met Gala Anna Wintour has arrived in a sleek black velvet tuxedo coat fixed with yellow and red flowers. She paired the striking Loewe coat with a white floor-length dress from the same designer and a decadent necklace.

    La La Anthony

    Donning a striking Alexander McQueen fitted black gown with a bold red ombre fishtail rose detailing, La La Anthony proved that the real key to Met Gala dressing is all in the way you style it. In this case, she paired a lace black longsleeve under her gown and decided on a diamante encrusted handbag to hold all the essentials.

    Ashley Graham

    Ashley made florals for spring groundbreaking in an elegant black mesh sequin gown with a sheer shawl that extended into a flowing train from Ludovic de Saint Sernin. The dress featured a fitted bodice adorned with floral appliques.
