George Kittle decided to accomplish new heights in his football career when he penned an NFL contract with the San Francisco 49ers in 2017. Before that, he played college football for the University of Iowa Hawkeyes.

George Kittle does not just enjoy professional success, he is blessed in his off-field life too thanks to the company of his beautiful and supportive wife.

George Kittle Bio: Age, Parents, Height

The 26-years-of-age football tight end was born as George Krieger Kittle on 9 October 1993 in Madison, Wisconsin. He later moved to Iowa at a young age with his parents, Bruce Kittle and Jan Krieger. His father, Bruce Kittle is a former college football player as well as a former college football coach.

About 6 feet and 4 inches (1.93 m) tall in height, George attended Iowa City West High School in Iowa City, Iowa. Later on, he moved to the Cedar Falls High School in Cedar Falls, Iowa, followed by Norman High School in Norman, Oklahoma. Eventually, he committed to the University of Iowa to play college football.

Career Stats, Contract

After a decent college career, George Kittle eyed a big-league start after the San Francisco 49ers selected him in the fifth round of the 2017 NFL Draft. Subsequently, on 4 May 2017, he signed a four-year, $2.69 million-worth contract with the 49ers that includes a signing bonus of $298,287.

George appeared first time in his 49ers jersey in his side’s season-opener against the Carolina Panthers. Though his team lost the game by 23-3 margin, he caught five passes for 27 yards. At the end of the rookie season, George finished with 43 receptions for 515 yards and two touchdowns in 15 games.

To the recent in 2019 season, he caught 8 passes for 103 yards in the 20-7 win over the Los Angeles Rams in week 6.

Who Is George Kittle’s Wife?

George Kittle shares a blissful marriage voyage with his beautiful wife, Claire Till. He walked down the aisle with his long-time girlfriend on 9 April 2019. This was after the pair decided to share an engagement ring in September 2018.

Like her husband, Claire is also a sports enthusiast. She played basketball at the University of Iowa, where the lovebirds first met their eyes. Eventually, their romance passed new heights and blessed them with an astounding bond.
