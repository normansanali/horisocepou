Kylie Rae Set To Wrestle On Main Event Television Taping Before WWE RAW

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  303 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kylie Rae was one of the first AEW signings when the promotion first started. However, she didn’t last long in AEW, as she requested her release from the company. Following her release from AEW, she worked in Impact Wrestling before moving to NWA. However, she hasn’t wrestled for NWA since earlier this year.

Recently, Kylie Rae’s name made headlines when it was reported that she underwent a week-long tryout at the WWE Performance Center. Now Fightful Select is reporting that Kylie is working a WWE Main Event ahead of WWE RAW’s taping against Dana Brooke.

AEW, IMPACT and NWA veteran Kylie Rae is working a WWE Main Event match ahead of tonight’s WWE Raw tapings. She’ll be working against Dana Brooke.

Kylie Rae was in Orlando last week at the WWE Performance Center for a week-long tryout. Those that we spoke to said that she seemed in high spirits. WWE held a more experienced tryout that included indie workers like KC Navarro, Dutch, and Vincent.

Kylie Rae was one of AEW’s first signings, but asked for her release for personal reasons in the summer of 2019, which AEW granted. She worked briefly for IMPACT Wrestling before moving to NWA. She’s not been seen there since earlier this year, but also worked a NJPW USA date which gives her the very unique distinction of working NJPW, WWE, IMPACT, NWA and AEW,” per Fightful Select.

It looks like Kylie Rae was able to impress the WWE trainers at the WWE Performance Center. We will have to wait and see when Kylie Rae will debut on either NXT or the main roster. Stay tuned to Ringside News as we keep you updated on this story as it develops,
