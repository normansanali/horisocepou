NJPW x STARDOM Historic X-Over Results (11/20/22): The Great Mutas Final NJPW Match

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  268 words  |  Aldo Pusey

On November 20, and STARDOM teamed up to present NJPW x STARDOM Historic X-Over.

In the main event, KAIRI faced Mayu Iwatani in a match that would crown the inaugural IWGP Women’s Champion. Plus, Will Ospreay defended the IWGP United States Championship against Shota Umino, and The Great Muta competed in his final NJPW match.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Alex Coughlin, Gabriel Kidd, Kevin Knight & Clark Connors def. Oskar Leube, Ryohei Oiwa, Kosei Fujita & Yuto Nakashima.
  • MIRAI def. Ami Sourei, Rina, Hazuki, Koguma, Momo Kohgo, Waka Tsukiyama, Saki Kashima, Ruaka, Saya Iida, Natsuko Tora, Hanan, Hina, Miyu Amasaki, and Super Strong Stardom Machine in a STARDOM Ranbo
  • CHAOS (Tomohiro Ishii, YOSHI-HASHI, Lio Rush & YOH) def. House of Torture (EVIL, SHO, Dick Togo & Yujiro Takahashi)
  • Queen’s Quest (Lady C, Saya Kamitani & AZM) def. Donna del Mondo (Himeka, Thekla & Mai Sakurai)
  • Zack Sabre Jr. & Giulia def. Tom Lawlor & Syuri
  • Tam Nakano, Taichi, Natsupoi, Yoshinobu Kanemaru def. Starlight Kid, El Desperado, Momo Watanabe & DOUKI
  • Hiroshi Tanahashi  & Utami Hayashishita def. Hirooki Goto & Maika
  • United Empire (TJP, Francesco Akira, Kyle Fletcher, Mark Davis & Gideon Grey) def. Los Ingobernables de Japon (Tetsuya Naito, Shingo Takagi, Hiromu Takahashi, SANADA & BUSHI)
  • Kazuchika Okada, The Great Muta & Toru Yano def. United Empire (Jeff Cobb, Great-O-Khan & Aaron Henare)
  • : Will Ospreay (c) def. Shota Umino
    • After the match, Kenny Omega challenged Will Ospreay to face him at Wrestle Kingdom 17
  • : KAIRI def. Mayu Iwatani
    • After the match, Tam Nakano challenged KAIRI to defend the title against her at Wrestle Kingdom 17
