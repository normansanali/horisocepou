Is EDT time zone same as EST?

Does EST change with daylight savings?

Background: Eastern Prevailing Time (EPT) is the time on an automatically updated clock, such as on a cellphone or computer. Clocks change from Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) to Eastern Standard Time (EST) on November 7th, 2021. At 02:00 EPT, clocks will “fall back” one hour to 01:00.
