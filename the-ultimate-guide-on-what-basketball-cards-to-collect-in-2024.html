The Ultimate Guide on What Basketball Cards to Collect in 2024

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  715 words  |  Martina Birk

Basketball cards have become more than just pieces of cardboard with player images on them. Over the years, they have evolved into valuable collectibles that capture the essence of the game and the greatness of its players. With the ever-changing landscape of the NBA and the card collecting industry, it's important to stay up-to-date on the latest trends and players to watch out for. In this guide, we will explore the ultimate basketball cards to collect in 2024.

1. The Rising Stars

Every year, new talents emerge in the NBA, capturing the attention of fans and collectors alike. In 2024, several young players are expected to make a significant impact on the league and become highly sought-after collectibles. Keep an eye out for players like Zion Williamson, Luka Dončić, Ja Morant, and LaMelo Ball. Their rookie cards are likely to be in high demand, and their value might skyrocket in the future.

2. The Legends

While collecting cards of rising stars is exciting, it's also important to remember the legends of the game. Players like LeBron James, Kevin Durant, Stephen Curry, and Giannis Antetokounmpo have already established themselves as all-time greats. Their cards from earlier years continue to hold significant value, and investing in their autographed or limited-edition cards can be a wise decision.

3. Limited Edition Sets

In recent years, card manufacturers have introduced various limited-edition sets that have gained immense popularity among collectors. These sets often feature unique designs, autographs, jersey relics, and even pieces of the basketball used in a game. Look out for sets like Panini Prizm, Topps Chrome, and Upper Deck Exquisite Collection, as they tend to hold their value well and offer a chance to own rare cards with high potential for future appreciation.

4. Hall of Famers

Collecting cards of Hall of Famers is a classic strategy that never goes out of style. Players like Michael Jordan, Magic Johnson, Larry Bird, and Kareem Abdul-Jabbar are basketball icons whose cards hold historical significance. Investing in their rookie cards or special edition cards can be a long-term investment that pays off in the future.

5. International Stars

Basketball has become a global sport, and international players are making their mark in the NBA. Collecting cards of international stars not only diversifies your collection but also gives you a chance to own pieces of history. Players like Dirk Nowitzki, Pau Gasol, Manu Ginobili, and Yao Ming have achieved tremendous success and have a devoted fan base. Their cards, especially those from their rookie years, can be valuable additions to any collection.

6. Special Inserts and Parallels

Card manufacturers often release special inserts and parallel sets that offer unique designs and limited availability. These cards can add depth and exclusivity to your collection. Look for cards with features like holographic foils, serial numbering, and rare variants. These cards often command higher prices and have the potential to appreciate in value over time.

7. Game-Worn Memorabilia

One of the most exciting types of basketball cards to collect is those featuring game-worn memorabilia. These cards contain pieces of jerseys, shorts, or even basketballs used in official NBA games. They provide a tangible connection to the game and can be highly valued by collectors. Keep an eye out for cards that feature patches, autographs, or multiple pieces of memorabilia, as they tend to be more sought after.

8. Graded Cards

When collecting basketball cards, it's important to consider their condition. Grading companies like PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator) and BGS (Beckett Grading Services) evaluate cards based on their condition and assign them a grade. Investing in graded cards ensures authenticity and increases their value. Look for cards graded above a certain level (e.g., PSA 9 or BGS 9.5) to ensure you're getting high-quality cards that have the potential to appreciate in value.

Basketball card collecting is a dynamic and exciting hobby that offers a unique way to connect with the game and its players. Whether you're collecting for personal enjoyment or as an investment, staying up-to-date on the latest trends and players is crucial. By following this ultimate guide, you'll be equipped with the knowledge to make informed decisions on what basketball cards to collect in 2024. Remember to research, diversify your collection, and enjoy the journey of building your own basketball card treasure trove.
