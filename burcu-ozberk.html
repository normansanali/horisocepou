On December 12, 1989, Burcu Ozberk was born. Burcu Ozberk is her full name, and Burcu is her nickname. She was born in the Turkish town of Eskisehir. Ozberk finished high school in the theatre department at the University of Hacettepe and made a TV debut in 2013 as Sultan of Hurician at Muhteşem Yüzyl. Her turning point came when she played Nazl Ylmaz in Güneşin Kzlar.

She also made her film debut in the film Direniş Karatay, where she played the character Türkan. Later, she starred in Kanal D’s comedy film Afili Aşk, in which she received a Golden Butterfly Award for Best Romantic Comedy Actress.
