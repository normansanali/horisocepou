I will be sharing the secrets behind creating the perfect molasses ginger cookies and why they pair so well with the creamy goodness of cream cheese frosting.

Why you will love these Soft Molasses Cookies:

The Magic of Molasses and Ginger: At the heart of every great gingerbread cookie lies the perfect blend of molasses and ginger. Molasses imparts a rich, deep flavor that adds complexity to the cookie. Its robust, slightly bitter undertones perfectly complement the warm and spicy kick of ground ginger and cloves.

The simple cream cheese frosting made with butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla bean paste makes these cookies so gourmet.

Ginger Molasses Cookie Ingredients:

This is the perfect soft and chewy spiced molasses cookie recipe made with fresh ingredients.

  • Butter — use unsalted butter. If you use salted butter, just reduce the salt amount to 1/4 teaspoon. I love to use European butter.
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Molasses — I suggest using Mother’s brand molasses.
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Spices: Cinnamon, Ginger, and Cloves — add the perfect amount of warmth and spice to the cookies.
  • Baking Soda, Baking Powder, and Salt
  • Sparkling Sugar

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • Butter — use barely softened butter.
  • Cream Cheese — I suggest using softened, full-fat cream cheese.
  • Powdered Sugar — may add more for a sweeter frosting.
  • Vanilla Extract or Vanilla Bean Paste

Why do Molasses Ginger Cookies pair so well with cream cheese frosting:

The pairing of molasses ginger cookies with cream cheese frosting is a match made in heaven. The creaminess of the frosting tempers the boldness of the molasses, ginger, and cloves, creating a harmonious balance of sweet and spice.

Cream cheese adds a tangy note that contrasts beautifully with the sweetness of the cookies, elevating the overall taste experience. The soft, chewy texture of the cookies meets the smooth creaminess of the frosting.

How to make Frosted Soft Gingerbread Cookies:

This is such an easy Christmas cookie recipe! Start with high-quality ingredients, including fresh ginger, good-quality molasses, and real butter. These ingredients will contribute to the rich, authentic flavor of your gingerbread cookies.

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar for 4 minutes until light and fluffy and pale in color.
  • Add egg, vanilla, and molasses and mix well.
  • Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves. Mix just until combined to avoid over-mixing the dough.
  • Place sugar into a small bowl. Roll dough into small balls and roll into sugar, coating well.
  • Place on a parchment paper lined baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes. Do not overbake these cookies as they are meant to be soft.
  • Make Cream Cheese Frosting. Whip until thick and creamy. Let the cookies cool before frosting. Generously frost with cream cheese frosting.

DO NOT OVERBAKE!

Keep a close eye on your cookies while baking. Overbaking can lead to a loss of softness and chewiness. Aim for slightly underbaked cookies that will continue to set as they cool.

Gingerbread Cookies with Cream Cheese Frosting FAQ’s

How do I know when they are done baking?

These are meant to be soft baked so they will have chewy centers and crisp edges. The cookies will begin to crinkle slightly on the edges but still be soft in the center.

Can I make this recipe without the frosting?

Of course! These are the perfect soft ginger molasses cookies and are delicious with or without the cream cheese frosting.
