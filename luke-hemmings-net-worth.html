To begin with, 5SOS started when Luke Hemmings, Michael Clifford and Calum Hood studied at the same school (Norwest Christian College), decided to join-up as a band and began posting videos on YouTube – the cover of the song “Next To You” (originally by Chris Brown and Justin Bieber) surpassed 600,000 views. Later, Ashton Irwin joined the band as a drummer, and together they reached more than five million views on their channel on YouTube. At the beginning of 2014, the band ‘s first single, “She Looks So Perfect” was released in the UK. A few months later, the band announced that their debut, self-titled album, which was released on 27th June 2014 in Europe and Australia, with more releases to come later. The album debuted in the top position on Billboard and reached the top position in another 13 countries, as well as the Top 10 in 26 more countries.
